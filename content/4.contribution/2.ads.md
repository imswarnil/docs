# Ad Component Showcase

This document demonstrates every `variant` and `displayStyle` of the `GoogleAd` component.

## Standard Display Ads

These are responsive units that fit most spaces.

### Horizontal

Great for banners at the top or bottom of a page.

::google-ad{variant="horizontal"}
::

### Vertical

Ideal for sidebars.

::google-ad{variant="vertical"}
::

### Square

Works well in grids or sidebars.

::google-ad{variant="square"}
::

---

## Native Ad Formats

These formats are designed to blend in with your content.

### In-Article

Place this between paragraphs in your long-form content. It adapts to the surrounding text.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Sed non risus. Suspendisse lectus tortor, dignissim sit amet, adipiscing nec, ultricies sed, dolor.

::google-ad{variant="in-article"}
::

Cras elementum ultrices diam. Maecenas ligula massa, varius a, semper congue, euismod non, mi. Proin porttitor, orci nec nonummy molestie, enim est eleifend mi, non fermentum diam nisl sit amet erat.

### In-Feed

Use this inside a list of items, like a blog index, to make it look like another list item.

- My First Blog Post
- Another Great Article
::google-ad{variant="in-feed"}
::
- The Ultimate Guide to Nuxt
- A Final Post Here

### Multiplex

A grid of sponsored articles. Best placed at the very end of your content to suggest further reading.

::google-ad{variant="multiplex"}
::

---

## Text Ad Variant

This variant uses a responsive display unit that is optimized to receive text ads from Google's network. It's great for placing inline with minimal distraction.

::google-ad{variant="text" displayStyle="minimal"}
::

---

## Display Styles

You can change the wrapper around any ad using the `displayStyle` prop.

### Standard (Default)

This is the default style with a padded, bordered, and background-colored frame.

::google-ad{variant="rectangle" displayStyle="standard"}
::

### Minimal

This style has no border or background. It's perfect for embedding ads directly into your content flow without a visible container.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Sed non risus.

::google-ad{variant="horizontal" displayStyle="minimal"}
::

Cras elementum ultrices diam. Maecenas ligula massa, varius a, semper congue, euismod non, mi.

### Full-Width

This style removes side margins and lets the ad's frame stretch to the full width of its parent container. Ideal for breaking up major sections of a page.

::google-ad{variant="leaderboard" displayStyle="full-width"}
::
